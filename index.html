Video Portfolio

Please enjoy some of my work below by clicking on the videos to watch them. Some have music edited into the videos and some don't. Filming and editing has been a passion of mine since I can remember. Filming and creating memories is what I enjoy most. I'm currently majoring in Digital Filmmaking and Video Production at the Art Institute. I currently also volunteer with my church in the media department every Wednesday and Sunday for church services. At those services I work with many types of cameras operating them for a large live production.

I've always had a passion for videography and photography. I would love to speak with you about helping with any events you have in mind. During these tough economic times, I'm more than happy to discuss/negotiate prices to anyone interested in my service. I promise you will get great work without spending a fortune for it. We will discuss what your looking for and fit it within a budget you're satisfied with. I look forward to working with you. Thank you!
